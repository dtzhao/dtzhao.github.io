About

        

          Hi! My name is David Zhao. I’m a product designer who has worked
          across consumer social and enterprise, data visualization and design
          tooling. I live in New York City with my laptop and lovely assortment
          of houseplants.
        

        

          Design appeals to me as an exercise in orienteering—of taste and
          aesthetic, incentive and behavior, efficiency and scale*—and for the present historical oddity as the territory looks to
          evolve faster than the compass and knapsack.
        

        

          *Why waste time ship wrong product when right design do trick?
